Maximum Temperature

Weather data from world cities were collected on January 5th, 2017. The maximum temperature was 120 degrees ferinheit. Between -20 and 20 latitudes, you will have warmer weather. This makes sense becuase those are areas closest to the equater. The further you get from the equater, the colder the temperature will be.
